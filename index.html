About

Full stack developer passionate about web accessibility, performance, and usability. Prior to being a developer, I provided services to adults and children in need for six years. My desire to help others is what drives me to make content that matters and have a positive impact on the world. I recently obtained a certificate in full stack development from Columbia University where I developed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, APIs, Node.js, React.js, Express.js, AWS S3, and My SQL. With each project, my goal is to improve overall user experience. I applied aspects of UX in a recent group project where I worked with a team of three to create a social media app for experienced and up-and-coming artist that helps users showcase their art and connect with other artists. I am excited to join a fast paced and hard-working team where I can utilize my skills to help improve user experience and web accessibility.

I develop websites with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Materialize, AWS S3, web APIs, and Node.js, focusing on accessibility, performance, and usability. When I'm not coding, you can find me biking, boxing, spending time with family, or learning new programing languages. I hope you enjoy my portfolio and look forward to hearing from you soon!

Work

Dinnection

AWS S3/ Node/ Express/
Sequelize/ Materialize/ Handlebars

Dinnection is a full-stack social media web app for artists to connect, view, and promote contemporary art. After creating an account and signing in, if users want to add new art to the app then they would click "New Post". The user's post is then stored in an AWS S3 bucket and the post displayed on the main page. Check out the Demo and Repo!



Travelers

Travelers is a full-stack web app for users planning travel within the United States during the COVID-19 pandemic. This app was created to help keep users up to date with the weather and COVID-19 cases, deaths, and risk. Check out the Demo and Repo!
